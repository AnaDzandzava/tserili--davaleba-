Global Open Font Family

In 2016, the geo data provider Here chose Fira Sans as their corporate typeface. Their need for a broader language coverage especially in map applications led them to commission us with a global script extension. Thanks to our great team of designers, consultants and technicians, the project was completed by 2017. Based on the Fira Sans 4.3 glyph set, FiraGO now supports Arabic, Devanagari, Georgian, Hebrew and Thai.

With this script support, FiraGO catches up with other global Open Source typefaces such as Noto. We decided to introduce FiraGO as a new product. That way, users of Fira Sans can also install FiraGO. For further information please have a look at the FiraGO project page. Feedback and ideas as well as funding for further development are always welcome!
